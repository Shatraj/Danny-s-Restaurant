Delight

DANNY’S RESTAURANT is open 6 days a week and is fully licensed. We offer comfortable seating for up to 50 people inside the restaurant and also offer some outside seating in the summer months. Advanced booking is recommended if you intend to visit but if you prefer a takeaway there is free delivery for orders costing £15 or more within a two-mile radius. For Food Allergies and Intolerances, please speak to a member of staff about the ingredients in your meal when making your order.
